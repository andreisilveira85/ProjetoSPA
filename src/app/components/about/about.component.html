About Me

Hello, I’m Adele Smith, a singer and songwriter from New York, deeply passionate about music and the stories that connect us. For as long as I can remember, music has been my most powerful form of expression, and through it, I share my experiences, dreams, and emotions with you.

One of my biggest inspirations comes from the series "SUTIS". I’m completely fascinated by the characters and the intertwined stories that explore the complexities of human relationships. Each episode brings me new perspectives on love, life, and the choices we make, and this is reflected in the songs I compose.

When I’m writing a song, I often think about the emotions and dilemmas I see in "SUTIS". I try to capture those subtle moments of joy, pain, love, and loss, and transform them into something that we can all feel and connect with. For me, every lyric and melody is an opportunity to create a bridge between what I feel and what you, my listeners, might be experiencing.

I’m very grateful for all the support I receive from you. Every time one of my songs touches someone’s heart, I feel like I’m fulfilling my purpose. My journey in music is for all of us, and I’m touched to know that my songs are a part of your lives.

If you’re also a fan of "SUTIS", or if you simply love music that speaks to the heart, I hope my songs bring you comfort, inspiration, and joy. Thank you for being with me on this musical journey.

With love,
Adele Smith
